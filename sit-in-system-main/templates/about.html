University of Cebu

Main Campus

The University of Cebu (UC) is a premier educational institution in Cebu City, Philippines. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs. The Main Campus is the flagship site, with facilities, faculty, and a strong student community.

UC promotes academic excellence, innovation, and holistic growth so graduates are ready for leadership and their professions. The mission is to equip students with knowledge, skills, and values that help them contribute to society and succeed in their careers.

University of Cebu Communities

Join the vibrant community at University of Cebu Main Campus and College of Computer Studies (CCS). Discover student organizations, publications, and activities that enrich campus life.

College of Computer Studies (CCS) Communities
  • Bits & Bytes Publication
    The official student publication of the College of Computer Studies, featuring articles on technology, programming, and student life.
  • CSS Student Council
    Represents CSS students in university governance and organizes academic and social events.
  • Programming Clubs
    Various coding and tech clubs including web development, mobile app development, and cybersecurity groups.
  • IT Competitions Team
    Participates in national and international programming contests and hackathons.
University of Cebu Main Campus Communities
  • Lakandiwa Publication
    The official student newspaper of UC Main Campus, covering news, features, and opinions.
  • Webmasters Athletics
    UC's sports teams competing in CESAFI, including basketball, football, swimming, and more.
  • Student Organizations
    Various clubs and societies for arts, culture, environment, and community service.
  • Fraternities & Sororities
    Recognized Greek-letter organizations promoting brotherhood/sisterhood and leadership.
  • Cultural Groups
    Dance troupes, choirs, and performing arts groups representing Cebuano culture.

For more information about joining communities, visit the Student Affairs Office or check the university website.
